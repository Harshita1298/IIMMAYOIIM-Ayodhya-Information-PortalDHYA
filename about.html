Dr. Harendra Kumar

Mobile :6392359004 / 9415402109

After getting associated with Indian Institute of Material Management (IIMM), I got the opportunity not only to improve my academic qualifications in Material Management (GDMM, PDMM & MBA (MM)) but also served IIMM Raebareli Branch in various capacities including Chairman of the Branch. The credit goes to our present National President Shri Lalit Raj Meena Sa who introduced me to IIMM during my service at SGPGIMS, of IIMM Ayodhya Branch is fully aware of its strength to strengthen the activities of IIMM.
